Social Studies Topics

Community Helpers

Learn about different community helpers such as doctors, teachers, and firefighters. Each plays a vital role in our society.
Did you know that teachers help shape the future by educating students? Firefighters not only put out fires but also educate communities about fire safety!

Festivals

Explore various festivals celebrated around the world, such as Diwali, Christmas, and Eid. Each festival has its unique customs and traditions.
For instance, during Diwali, people light oil lamps, while Christmas is celebrated with trees, lights, and gifts. Eid is marked by feasting and giving to those in need.

Geography

Discover the geographical features of our planet, including mountains, rivers, and continents.
The Earth has seven continents: Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe, and Australia. Each continent has its own unique landscapes and cultures!
