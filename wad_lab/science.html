Science Topics

Animals

Learn about different animals and their habitats. For example, lions live in the savanna, while penguins thrive in cold climates.
Did you know that elephants are the largest land animals? They can weigh up to 6 tons!

Plants

Explore how plants grow and the parts of a plant. Plants need sunlight, water, and nutrients to survive.
Additionally, plants play a crucial role in producing oxygen and providing food for humans and animals.

Weather

Understand different weather patterns and how they affect our daily lives. Learn about sunny, rainy, and snowy days!
Weather changes can influence our activities, from choosing what to wear to planning outdoor events.

The Solar System

Discover the planets in our solar system and their unique features. Did you know Jupiter is the largest planet?
It has a giant storm called the Great Red Spot that has been raging for hundreds of years!
